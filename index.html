About Us

Our Vision

New Life Children's Home was established in 2016 with a vision of creating a revolutionary safe haven for all vulnerable children. Our goal is to provide a nurturing and secure environment where children can thrive and overcome the challenges they face. We envision a future where every child has access to education, safety, good ethics, and self-empowerment.

Our Mission

At New Life Children's Home, our mission is to create an environment that is conducive to the holistic development of every child under our care. We are committed to ensuring their education, safety, well-being, and personal growth. As a non-profit organization, we work tirelessly to find innovative solutions that provide affordable housing and comprehensive support services for vulnerable children.

Our Success Stories

Our Baby Safe is working! We were able to save the life of this little girl when her mother had a safe and anonymous place to surrender her child. Too many babies are left in dire situations, open to the elements to die a slow death, due to the social taboo of Baby Safes.

A little boy of 5 months old who was only given water for his hunger pains was brought into our care. Initially unable to digest formula and severely underweight, we are happy to report that after 1 month in our care, his progress improves daily, and he is even starting to fill out his baby-grow!
