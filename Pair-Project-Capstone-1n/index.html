About us

New Life Children's Homewas established in 2016 with a vision of creating a revolutionary safe haven for all vulnerable children.
Our mission is to create an enviroment that is condusive to education, safety, good ethics and self empowerment for all our charges.
As New Life Children's Home is a non-profit organization, we needed to think out-side-the-box and strategize how we could serve our mission and still provide affordable housing to those in need.
We were overwhelmed by the response from the community, and together with the church and our sponsors, we have created a Container-village from recycled containers that have been converted into fully operational facilities consisting of:

  • Bedrooms
  • Bathrooms
  • Kitchen
  • Disability friendly unit
  • Play rooms
  • Social spaces

We aim to encorporate a further 20 children over the next 3 years. In order to provide the assistance so greatly needed, we urgently require the generosity of you, our supporters. We are in desperate need of donations and funding from corporate and individual members so that together we can achieve this outcome.

Our success stories

Our Bay Safeis working! We were able to save the life of this little girl when her mother had a safe and anonymous place to surrender her child. Too many babies are left in dire situations, open to the elements to die a slow death, due to the social taboo of Baby Safes.
We feel that providing this option to people unable to care for their baby, ensures those babies are surrendered to our Baby safeare the lucky ones not abandoned in toilets, park, velds and dumps.

A little boy of 5 months old who was only given water for his hunger pains was brought in to our care. He was unable to digest formula when he arrived and was very under weight. We are happy to report after 1 month in our care, his progress improves daily and he is even starting to fill out his baby-grow!
